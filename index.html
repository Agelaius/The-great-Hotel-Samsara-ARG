The easiest way to investigate these sites is to look at the code, but you can also hit ctrl+A to highlight every element on the page at once, revealing anything hidden.

Something to note about the red background before proveeding- it's actually been changed a few times, cycling from red, to blue, green, black, white, and then red again.

If you visit the site, there is immediately a bunch of noise. There's one part of a script here that pans the audio to a humming noise left and right based on the mouse position on the screen vertically, and another that changes the pitch of another tone based on the horizontal mouse position. On top of that, a series of sounds are generated randomly based on the mouse's movement, so in total there's a bunch of funky noises going on here. This sound script is shared across most of the main pages.
